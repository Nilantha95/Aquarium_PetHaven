About Us

The Petshop is the leading specialist retailer of pet goods, accessories and aquatics in the Middle East. Founded in 2011 by Charlotte and Anders Jorgensen, the company grew from their small villa in Dubai into the largest retailer of pet food and supplies in the region.

With five stores around the UAE, including a 54,000-square-foot Megastore in Dubai Investment Park, and an online store offering a range of 12,000+ products, we deliver straight to your doorstep! We offer same-day delivery in Dubai if you order before 9 AM & free delivery all over UAE if your order is above AED 100. The Petshop offers quality and convenience. All of which are available to you in just a few clicks.

We are dedicated to bringing joy to the lives of pets and their pet parents every single day. Apart from our extensive range of products we also offer a wide range of services to help support you on your journey as a loving pet parent. These include pet relocation services as well as mobile and in-store grooming for cats and dogs.

Pet Relocation

Whether you are travelling to the UAE or moving with your pet across the world, our dedicated Pet Relocation team are available to guide you every step of the way. Take away the stress of travelling and enjoy this new chapter with your pet instead.

Giving Back to the Community

As an organization of animal lovers, we are always looking at ways to support the local animal rescue community. We host regular weekend adoption days and have had the privilege of helping hundreds of dogs, cats & rabbits find their forever homes.
